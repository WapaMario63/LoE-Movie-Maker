About [LoEWCT]

[LoEWCT] is made from the original LoE Private Server from mlkj/tux3 on github: https://github.com/tux3/LoE-PrivateServer

[LoEWCT] is made by WapaMario63 owner of The Wapa Castle Team.

[LoEWCT] stands for Legends of Equestria, Wapa Castle Team Edition, the square brackets is just for making it look fancy.

[LoEWCT] is in NO way related to the development of LoE with the LoE team.

[LoEWCt] is made with the purpose of making server owners jobs easier and user-friendlier WHILE normal player's experiences get richer with additional content that the server is capable of adding and/or doing. Plugins is an example.

For all of LoE's news and announcements, go to http://www.legendsofequestria.com/
